# Welcome to WatchToku

WatchToku is your number one stop for enjoying your favorite Tokusatsu shows! Dive into an exciting world where classic genres come alive with action, drama, and spectacular special effects!

## Project Description
WatchToku allows fans of Tokusatsu to easily access and watch a variety of shows from the genre. Whether you're a long-time fan or new to the world of Tokusatsu, WatchToku is designed to cater to your viewing needs.

## Features
- **Extensive Library**: A wide selection of Tokusatsu shows available for streaming.
- **User-Friendly Interface**: Navigate easily to find your favorite shows and discover new ones.
- **Watch List**: Create a personalized watch list to keep track of your favorite series.
- **Community Ratings**: Rate and review shows to help fellow viewers make informed choices.

## How to Use
1. **Browse Shows**: Start by browsing our extensive library of Tokusatsu shows.
2. **Select a Show**: Click on a show to view details, including episodes and descriptions.
3. **Create an Account**: Sign up to create a personalized watch list and participate in community ratings.
4. **Watch and Enjoy**: Start watching your selected shows and immerse yourself in the Tokusatsu universe!

Join us at WatchToku and relive the excitement of your favorite shows!
